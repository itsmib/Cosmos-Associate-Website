# A garden address, a forever home

Ruby Garden is our newest gated layout in the heart of Karaikal — 42
DTCP / KPA / PPA-approved plots laid out around a landscaped central park, designed for
families who want the calm of a garden community without leaving the city.

## Why Ruby Garden

Every plot here is **clear-title, DTCP / KPA / PPA approved, and ready to build on day one**.
We've put 30 ft internal roads on every side, full underground drainage, and
solar-powered street lighting so the layout stays lit through the evening.

## Location & connectivity

A short drive from Karaikal town centre, schools, hospitals, and the beach.
Every plot enjoys a quiet, leafy frontage — but you're still minutes from
everything that matters.
